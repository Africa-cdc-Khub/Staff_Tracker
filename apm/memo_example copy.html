Request for PHEOC Infrastructure Inspection and Installation of ICT Equipment and Accessories

Reference is made to the approval of AU/CDC/EPR/IM/5439.23 Supply, Delivery and Installation of ICT Equipment and Accessories identified in the earlier memorandum dated 14 September 2023. The purpose of this memo is to request approval for conducting inspections and installations of critical ICT equipment and accessories for the 23 Public Health Emergency Operations Centers (PHEOCs).

PHEOCs play a vital role in managing public health emergencies, serving as hubs for resource coordination, communication, and collaboration with key stakeholders. The earlier assessment of these PHEOCs revealed several infrastructure gaps requiring urgent attention.

Scope aligned with AfDB-funded PHEOC capacity strengthening

  1. Office Equipment: Printers, scanners, whiteboards, and other administrative essentials.
  2. ICT Infrastructure: Servers, laptops, smartboards, TVs, biometric systems, and networking accessories.
  3. Networking Solutions: Routers, docking stations, AVS voltage monitors, and related equipment.
  4. Safety and Security: Cameras, fire extinguishers, access controls, and power backup systems.
  5. Furniture: Workstations, ergonomic chairs, executive desks, and shelves.
  6. Software: Antivirus licenses and office productivity tools.

Inspection Checklist

  1. Pre-Inspection of Equipment
    • Verify that all equipment is received in good condition.
    • Check for any visible damage upon unpacking; ensure that packaging seals are intact.
  2. On-Site Testing
    • Conduct electrical checks to confirm there are no frayed cables or wiring issues.
    • Test functionality of each piece of equipment.
  3. Foundation Checks
    • Ensure that the installation site has adequate ventilation, power, and space.
    • Confirm that environmental conditions are suitable for the equipment.
  4. Technicians
    • Schedule qualified technicians for installation based on their availability.
    • Optimize routes to minimize travel time to the installation site.

Installation Timeline

  • Initial Inspection: first week of March 2025.

We kindly request approval to:

  1. Conduct inspections of PHEOC infrastructure to finalize requirements.
  2. Proceed with the installation of the approved ICT equipment and accessories.
  3. Engage local service providers for site-specific installations.

The cost estimate to cover this activity is USD 205,270, approved by the AfDB for the PHEOC project from budget code CDC0603015MB.

Submitted for your consideration and approval.

Subject:
Request for Regional Infrastructure Inspection and ICT Installations

This memo seeks approval to conduct inspections and installations of ICT equipment and accessories for 18 Regional Emergency Centers. Previous assessments revealed gaps in infrastructure requiring urgent action.

Scope

  1. Office Tools: Printers, scanners, projectors.
  2. ICT Hardware: Laptops, servers, biometric devices.
  3. Networking: Routers, switches, cabling.
  4. Security: Cameras, fire equipment, backup systems.
  5. Furniture: Desks, chairs, storage.

Inspection Checklist

  • Verify delivery condition of equipment.
  • Conduct site readiness assessments.
  • Perform functionality tests.

Budget Summary

Estimated cost: USD 178,450 from budget code OPS042530MB.
